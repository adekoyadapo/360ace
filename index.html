What we do...

IT Infrastructure

We assist in designing and implementing the foundation of all IT systems; typically physical and virtual components, also involving the software and network components. We are focused on getting your infrastructure architecture to best practice(s).

Infrastructure As Code

Specializing in the automation of infrastructure components by creating templates inform of codes; with physical and virtual components specialized in declarative formats and stored with idempotence.

Continous Deployment

Emphasis on focusing on getting application delivery to the required environments with least resistance and speed leveraging on the cloud-native solution and best deployment and delivery tools available.

DevOps & SRE

Covering the entire product and application lifecycle and making it continuous; Leveraging on AGILE methodologies With SRE to ensure measurements and metrics are designed around the infrastructure and application to ensure a highly available product.

Configuration Management

Ensure maintenance of infrastructure leveraging on a templatize and automated configuration(s) to ensure consistency across multiple systems with respect to its desired state and changes made over time.

Networking

We assist in providing the design and implement the network communication requirements for your Infrastructure. Ranging from IP Address planning, routing, switching, VPN, cloud networking and hybrid connectivity

Cloud Architecture

Engage in consultation, pulling information gathered to design and implement the resource components of the Cloud. This is all in the drive towards digital transformation and meeting business requirements whilst leveraging on best practice framework and minimizing OPEX.

IT Infrastructure Security

Putting into perspective the requirements for protecting the infrastructure by implementing multiple layers of security. Also looking at various security frameworks to help foster a complete assessment and implement remediation needed to protect the infrastructure.

Training and Consultation

We will provide guidance towards digital transformation and bring in experts to meet varying requirements for training the team.

Development Planning

Planning out the continuous deployment and delivery, is our intent to ensure that your development process ends in the integration of deployment process to the required environments.

Infrastructure Monitoring

The process of collecting and analyzing data from IT Infrastructure, systems and process is required in achieving the desired state of the infrastructure as its designed to meet the demands it is intended for. With this we will help in facilitating the process required for meeting the monitoring demands.

Continuous Integration

One of the DevOps process requires automating the integration of code changes from multiple contributors into a single software project. This process can be tedious and requires careful planning and implementation to ensure its automated seamlessly.
